📖 HOW TO PLAY
🎯 GOAL
Escape the Forgotten Land. To do that you must collect 4 boss keys and use them at the EXIT GATE. The gate is marked green on your radar.
The gate is far north-east of spawn. You cannot use it without all 4 keys.
📋 STEP-BY-STEP
1.Find 🗺 Map Fragments — glowing blue circles on the radar. Walk into them to collect. You need all 4 fragments to unlock boss rooms.
2.Enter BOSS ROOMS (red circles on radar). A boss spawns — kill it and grab the boss key it drops.
3.Collect all 4 keys (one per boss room). Your key count shows top-right.
4.Walk to the EXIT GATE (green on radar / map). With all keys it opens — you escape!
Shortcut tip: The OBJECTIVE ARROW appears every 30s showing where to go next. You can also press M to toggle the full map.
🔫 COMBAT
•Shoot: Left click / SPACE / FIRE button
•Reload: R key / RELOAD button
•Switch weapon: 1/2/3 keys or E/F or ◄/► buttons
•Pistol: Balanced, fast. Shotgun: Powerful close range. Sniper: Long range, slow.
Bosses have huge HP. Use the Sniper at range or Death Mark ability to deal double damage.
⚡ ABILITIES
Glowing orbs scattered on the map grant you a special ability. They appear on radar. Only one ability can be held at a time.

•Shadow Step 👣 — Become invisible for 3s. Creatures lose sight of you.
•Berserker Rush ⚡ — 3× speed + 2× fire rate for 4s.
•Iron Shield 🛡 — No damage for 4s.
•Nova Burst 💥 — Stuns all nearby enemies.
•Death Mark ☠ — 2× weapon damage for 6s — best for bosses!
Press Q (keyboard) or the ABILITY button (mobile) to activate. Has a cooldown.
🧭 RADAR & MAP
•White dot = you. Red dots = boss rooms. Blue dots = map fragments. Coloured dots = team mates.
•Creatures only show on radar when chasing you, spawning, or during the Dark Pulse.
•Press M to toggle the large map panel (bottom-left).
The DARK PULSE happens every 30s — all creatures briefly glow red through darkness. Use this to spot threats.
👥 MULTIPLAYER
1.Host clicks CREATE ROOM — share the room ID and password with friends.
2.Friends enter the ID + password in JOIN ROOM and join.
3.All players mark ready. Host clicks START GAME.
4.All players share the same world. Help each other fight bosses!
Team mates appear as glowing coloured characters with name tags. Their positions sync in real time.
💀 CREATURES
Creatures patrol in the dark. Stepping into their flashlight cone raises their alert bar. When full — they chase!

•Runner — Fast charger. Move sideways to dodge.
•Stalker — Can teleport near you. Keep distance.
•Screamer — Wail slows you. Kill it fast.
•Juggernaut — Massive HP, stun stomp. Use sniper.
•Mimic — Disguised as pickup. Don't get tricked!
If a creature starts chasing, break its line of sight behind a wall. After 5 seconds it gives up.
🩺 SURVIVAL TIPS
•♥ Health pickups (red hearts) are scattered around. Watch your HP bar.
•⬡ Ammo pickups (yellow hexagons) refill reserve. Always reload after a fight.
•📜 Notes/clues give lore hints and may point to hidden paths.
•Hold Shift to sprint (uses stamina). Useful to escape creatures.
When you die you can RESPAWN at spawn point — your progress (keys, map fragments) is saved!
